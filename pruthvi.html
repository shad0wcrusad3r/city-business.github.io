Why We Use Dijkstra's Algorithm

Dijkstra's Algorithm is used to calculate the shortest path between the restaurant and the customer's location, ensuring:

  • Timely delivery of food.
  • Fuel-efficient routing for delivery agents.
  • Cost-effective operations in urban settings.
