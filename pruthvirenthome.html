Why We Use Prim's Algorithm

Prim’s Algorithm is used to calculate the optimal property allocation for rental agents, ensuring:

  • Efficient allocation of agents to properties.
  • Minimization of travel time for agents.
  • Cost-effective operations for managing rental properties.

Application of Efficient Algorithms

By applying algorithms such as route optimization, data analytics, and machine learning, house rental platforms can:

  • Reduce operational costs and energy consumption.
  • Promote affordable housing by optimizing search results for tenants.
  • Enhance urban planning by improving land usage and ensuring better housing development.
  • Support sustainable consumption by utilizing properties more efficiently and responsibly.
