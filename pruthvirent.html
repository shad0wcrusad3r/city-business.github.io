Why We Use Prim's Algorithm

Prim’s Algorithm is used to calculate the optimal property allocation for rental agents, ensuring:

  • Efficient allocation of agents to properties.
  • Minimization of travel time for agents.
  • Cost-effective operations for managing rental properties.
