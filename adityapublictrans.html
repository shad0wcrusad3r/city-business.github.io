Why We Use Dijkstra's Algorithm

Dijkstra's Algorithm is used to calculate the shortest path to the nearest public transport stop from a commuter's location, ensuring:

  • Improved convenience for commuters.
  • Reduced overall travel time.
  • Encouraged adoption of public transportation systems.
