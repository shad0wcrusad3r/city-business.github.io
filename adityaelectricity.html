Why We Use Kruskal's Algorithm

Kruskal's Algorithm is used to construct a Minimum Spanning Tree (MST) for the electricity distribution network, ensuring:

  • Efficient connection of all power stations, substations, and distribution points.
  • Minimized energy loss due to transmission line resistance.
  • Cost-effective infrastructure setup.
